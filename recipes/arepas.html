Arepas

Description

Arepas are amazing. You should probably eat them.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Water
  • Cooking Oil
  • Masarepa/Areparina (Corn Flour)

Steps

  1. Dissolve salt in water; slowly add masarepa while stirring with spoon
    • Mix white and yellow masarepa
    • Add more water if edges crack once dough is formed
  2. Scoop out fist-size portions, form a ball, once dough has rested for 5 minutes
  3. Softly smash into discs
  4. Pan-fry in cooking oil to form the crust
    • Deep blackening is okay
    • Finish in the oven to ensure centers are cooked through
    Optional:
    1. Split open discs and add shredded chicken
    2. Add shredded cheese
