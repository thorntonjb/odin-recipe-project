Pabellon

Description

This is describing Pabellon Criollo. It is a Venezuelan dish with fried plantains, shredded steak, white rice, and black beans. It can be eaten as its own dish in a bowl/on a plate. It can also be put into a tortilla to make a Pabellon Taco.

Ingredients

  • Flank steak cut in 4 pieces
  • Chopped onion
  • Water
  • Chopped red bell peppers
  • GOYA Black Bean Soup
  • GOYA Frozen Ripe Plantain
  • CANILLA Extra Long Grain Rice

Steps Please read all steps before cooking

  1. In saucepan, combine meat, onion, bouillon and water. Bring to boil, lower heat and simmer until very tender (about 1Â½ hours).
    • Add more water as necessary to keep meat covered.
    • Allow meat to cool in broth.
    • When cool enough to handle, shred meat. Reserve 1 cup of broth.
  2. In meantime, puree onion, red pepper and garlic in food processor.
  3. In skillet heat oil on medium high. Add shredded meat and lightly brown. Add pureed vegetables, tomato sauce, Sazon, oregano, Adobo and reserved broth. Bring to boil, lower heat and simmer until mixture dries about 20 minutes.
  4. Arrange meat, black beans, rice and plantain on platter and serve.
